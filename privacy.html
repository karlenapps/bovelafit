Privacy Policy

for Bovela Fit League · Last updated: November 6, 2025

We keep things simple: your training data lives on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no ads, no tracking.

Summary

No data collection We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal information.

On-device only Your programs, sessions, and settings are stored locally on your iPhone.

No third parties The app does not integrate analytics SDKs, ad networks, or external tracking tools.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect names, emails, or contact details.
  • We do not collect workout metrics off your device.
  • We do not collect usage analytics or diagnostic identifiers.
  • We do not use cookies or similar trackers.

On-Device Processing

The app processes your inputs (like interval structure or tempo targets) locally to build and run sessions. This data remains on your device unless you manually share it (for example, by exporting or backing up your phone).

Permissions

The app may use the following system features, strictly for functionality:

  • Haptics: to provide subtle feedback during sessions if enabled in Settings.

These features do not transmit data to any server.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly target or collect data from children. Because the app performs on-device storage only, there is no server-side collection of personal information.

Third-Party Services

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used. There is no cloud backend.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored on your device. You can remove it at any time by using the in-app option to erase history or by deleting the app from your device.

Security

Because your data stays on your device, it benefits from your device’s built-in security, including your passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID where applicable.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect product or legal changes. If we make material updates, we will adjust the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please reach out via the contact method listed on our App Store page.
